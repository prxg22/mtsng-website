About

Brazilian designer based in São Paulo. I worked as a product designer at Intermarine Yachts, a leading brazilian shiyard focused on luxury yachts, specializing in the premium segment and high-ends goods.

In 2014 I moved to NZ where I took a project management course and worked at the entertainment sector, seeking one of my big dreams, learn how to sail.

Almost 3 years and 16 contries later, I’ve returned to Brazil and was invited back to the Intermarine, now led by one of the youngest CEOs in the country, the company is heading to exportationand seeking to modernize production.

I believe in a strategic design able to cause a positive impact beyond the product value and that constantly pursue relevant impact not only on the final client but to the whole production cycle. Design to improve people’s lives. For the user beyond the costumer.
